About Igalapedia

Preserving the Igala Language for Future Generations

A comprehensive open-source project focused on preserving and promoting the Igala language and culture through technology.

Our Mission

Igalapedia is dedicated to creating a comprehensive digital encyclopedia that preserves the rich linguistic and cultural heritage of the Igala people. We believe that language is not just a means of communication, but a vessel carrying history, wisdom, and identity.

Through modern technology and community collaboration, we're building a platform that makes Igala language accessible to learners, preserves it for future generations, and celebrates the cultural diversity it represents.

Our Story

Igalapedia was born from a deep passion for preserving African languages in the digital age. Inspired by the groundbreaking IgboAPI - the first African Language API - we recognized the urgent need to document and digitize the Igala language before it fades from active use.

The Igala people, predominantly found in Kogi State, Nigeria, have a rich linguistic and cultural heritage. However, like many indigenous languages, Igala faces the challenge of declining usage among younger generations. Igalapedia aims to reverse this trend by making the language accessible, engaging, and relevant in the modern world.

We're building more than just a dictionary - we're creating a living archive that combines language documentation with cultural preservation, educational resources, and community engagement.
